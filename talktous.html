Useful Hotlines

Struggling? Get free, confidential support from a real human over phone, text or webchat. (24H)

  • 0161 681 6887 - Phone Consultation - an informal discussion to find out about your needs. You will be asked about your condition, when it started, what your symptoms are and how you are coping. If you have had an accident, you will be asked about how it happened, about your injury and how it affects your everyday life. You will also be asked about any treatment you have had so far.

  • 111 - NHS - Call 111 if you think you need medical help right now. Answer questions about your symptoms and you will be told what to do next.

  • Ask a Physio - Spend less time suffering and more time doing what you love. Physiofusion's Ask A Physio service is a free and confidential service designed to provide you with advice and information on your problem before you book an appointment.

Take the online Physiotherapy Check

At Physiocheck.co.uk you can check your symptoms by means of an interactive physiotherapy check. By answering the questions in the physiotherapy check and performing the tests, your symptoms will be compared to the most common physiotherapeutic conditions. Please do not consider this test as a diagnosis.
